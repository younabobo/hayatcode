Why Hayat Code!

Medical errors are the main reason of a serious amount of deaths especially during emergency cases, mainly due to the lack of information about the patients, like their medical records, allergies and many more

Hayatcode provides clients with Qrcode which will be a reference to their entire medical history. They would have the ability to access, edit and even share their information, track health conditions and get better healthcare solutions.


The result is fewer medical errors, avoid death cases related to ignorance, earlier treatment at lower costs.
